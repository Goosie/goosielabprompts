Geese live in Bitcoin time. Every goose gets a blockbirth (unique block nr ~2009–2015 era) and a deathblock (blockbirth + ~1.5M blocks = 30 years). Their age is counted in blocks, current block always comes from Blocky via the relay.

Every goose gets a lastwill. Users can put themselves on it and choose what they receive when the goose dies — a Nostr note, a Honkference invite, sats.

Special welfare goosies represent good causes. Apps split a % of their sat income to them. Later: users can create their own welfare goose.

Goose profile fields: `blockbirth`, `interests`, `lifestoflyto`.
